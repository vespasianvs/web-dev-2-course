My Learning Journey

Since deciding to move back into IT, an industry that I love and have missed for many years, it has been a challenge to find out what skills are needed and where my skills need updating. After speaking to several recruitment agents, I decided to retrain as a Web Developer. This was the role that I had before I left IT, so it made sense to mave back to it.

Since starting my training, I have completed certifications with Microsoft and FreeCodeCamp and have completed the 'Complete Web Developer Course 2.0' on Udemy. I am currently planning to take my Microsoft Certification in HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript (70-480) shortly with the aim eventually of achieving my MCSD: App Builder.

On my journey, I have had the opportunity to work on a variety of 'demo' projects, some of which you will see below.
